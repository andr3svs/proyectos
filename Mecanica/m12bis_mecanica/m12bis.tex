\section{Introduction and Objectives}
% GUIDELINE: Briefly explain the physics behind the experiment. 
% Mention the model being tested (e.g., Newton's Second Law for a damped oscillator).
The primary objective of this experiment is to study the mechanical scattering of particles, specifically focusing on the behavior of a projectile particle interacting with a target particle in a Rutherford-like potential. The experiment aims to:
In the first part, we first discuss qualitatively the trajectories in function of the initial energy, then we quantitatively analyze the scattering process by measuring the trajectories of the particles under various initial conditions, determining the interaction constant of the hill-like structure, from now on the hill and the true position of the center of forces.
In the second part, we will discuss the behavior of the system when the projectile particle is launched into a potential well, analyzing the resulting trajectories and comparing them with theoretical predictions.
